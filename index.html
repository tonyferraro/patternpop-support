Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?
Tap groups of 3 or more tiles with the same pattern to clear them from the board. The bigger the group, the more points you earn. Reach the target score before you run out of moves to complete the level.
Are there ads in Pattern Pop?
No. Pattern Pop is completely ad-free and always will be. You paid for the game — you deserve an uninterrupted experience.
How many levels are there?
Pattern Pop launches with 100+ levels of increasing difficulty. More levels will be added in future updates at no extra cost.
What does the star rating mean?
Each level awards 1–3 stars based on how efficiently you reach the target score. Finishing with moves to spare earns you more stars. Try to three-star every level!
What is the Tip Jar?
The Tip Jar is a completely optional way to support ongoing development. It's not required for any gameplay — just a way to say thanks if you enjoy the game.
I'm stuck on a level. Any tips?
Look for large groups rather than small ones — a group of 5 scores much more than two groups of 3. Also, focus on clearing tiles from the bottom of the board first to create chain reactions as new tiles fall in.
Does Pattern Pop require an internet connection?
No. Pattern Pop works fully offline. Play anywhere, anytime.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email [email protected] with a description of what happened, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots or screen recordings are super helpful too.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The Short Version

Pattern Pop does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Period.

Data Collection

Pattern Pop does not collect any personal information, usage analytics, crash reports, device identifiers, or location data. The game does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Your game progress (levels completed, high scores, star ratings) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

In-App Purchases

Pattern Pop offers optional Tip Jar purchases processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Payment information is handled by Apple and is never accessible to us. We do not receive or store any financial data.

Third-Party Services

Pattern Pop does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Pattern Pop does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy ever changes, updates will be posted on this page with a revised date. Given that the app collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
